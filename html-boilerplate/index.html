Lists

Unordered lists

To make unordered list use ul tag, this lists your items in bullet points.

  • Item A
  • Item C
  • Item E

Ordered lists

Use ol to make ordered lists, this lists your items using numbers.

  1. Item no. 1
  2. Item no. 2
  3. Item no. 3
